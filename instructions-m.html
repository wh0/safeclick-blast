How to Play

Defend your space datacenter from asteroids!

  • Click the asteroid when it lights up as a red asteroid.
  • You get 3 points every time when clicking on a lit up asteroid.
  • You lose 1 point every time when clicking on a regular asteroid.

Try to maximize your score in 2 minutes.

Items

Lit up asteroid: +3 point

Regular asteroid: -1 point

Server (NOT interactive)

Tip

If you click on a lit up asteroid overlapped by a server, you still get 3 points
