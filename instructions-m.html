This game is about chasing a pesky asteroid around your space datacenter.

How to Play

  • You start out with just your servers (not interactive) and the asteroid (stationary).
  • Click near the asteroid to start the chase. The asteroid will begin to move.
  • The asteroid will light up every few seconds.
  • Click the asteroid when it is lit up, which gives you 3 points. Obviously you'll have to chase it around.
  • Note: if the asteroid is behind a server and you click the server, it still counts.
  • If you accidentally click the asteroid when it is not lit, you lose 1 point.
  • Try to maximize your score in 3 minutes.
  • After 3 minutes, you will be redirected to a page containing a randomly generated password. Submit this password to complete the HIT on Mechanical Turk.
