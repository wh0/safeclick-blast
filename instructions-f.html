How to Play

Defend your space datacenter from asteroids!

  • Asteroids will appear in your datacenter.
  • Asteroids release minerals, causing chaos in your datacenter.
  • An arrow shows the direction of the next mineral release.
  • Catch the minerals before they disappear.

Try to catch as many minerals as possible in 2 minutes.

Items

Asteroid (not interactive)

Mineral: 3 points

Server (not interactive)
