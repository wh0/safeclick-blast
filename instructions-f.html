In this game, you defend a space datacenter from asteroids.

How to Play

Defend your space datacenter from asteroids!

  • You start out with just your servers (not interactive).
  • An asteroid will appear in your datacenter.
  • An arrow (>>>) will spin around the asteroid and stop.
  • When the arrow stops, a mineral will shoot out in the direction of the arrow.
  • Click the mineral quickly, giving your 3 points. Otherwise, it disintegrates.
  • Defend the datacenter for 2 minutes. Try to catch as many minerals as possible in this time.
  • After 2 minutes, you will be redirected to a page containing a randomly generated password. Submit this password to complete the HIT on Mechanical Turk.
