This game is about clicking and right-clicking. Rhythmically.

How to Play

  • After you read these instructions and click the link at the bottom of this page, the game will start out paused.
  • Put your mouse on the bottom left on the sensor to start the game.
  • Keep your mouse on the sensor, or your clicks will not register.
  • Blocks will start falling from the top of the screen towards a set of holes .
  • When this block falls into the left hole, click the left mouse button.
  • When this block falls into the right hole, click the right mouse button.
  • If you time your clicks right, you can get one point per block.

Note: If your mouse is "left handed," the commands will probably be reversed. Sorry about that.
