How to Play

Click and right-click. Rhythmically.

  • Keep your mouse on the click sensor to play.
  • Time your clicks for when the falling blocks fit in the holes.
  • If your mouse is "left handed," the commands will probably be reversed. Sorry about that.

Try to maximize your score through the click sequence.

Items

Sensor: keep your mouse here to play

L-block: click the primary mouse button

R-block: click the secondary mouse button
