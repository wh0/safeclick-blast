In this game, you defend a space datacenter from asteroids.

How to Play

  • You start out with just your servers (not interactive).
  • Asteroids will appear.
  • Click on the asteroids to destroy them, giving you 1 point.
  • Destroying an asteroid will release a mineral .
  • Click on the mineral before it disintegrates, giving you 3 points.
  • Defend the datacenter for 2 minutes.
